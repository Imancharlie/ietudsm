About IET

Institution of Engineers Tanzania (IET)

University of Dar es Salaam Chapter

Our Mission

The Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) at the University of Dar es Salaam is dedicated to inspiring, informing, and influencing the global engineering and technology community to engineer a better world.

What We Do
  • Provide professional development opportunities for engineering students
  • Organize technical workshops, seminars, and conferences
  • Foster networking among students, faculty, and industry professionals
  • Promote innovation and research in engineering and technology
  • Support career development and job placement
  • Encourage community service through engineering projects
Membership Benefits
  • Access to exclusive workshops and training
  • Networking opportunities
  • Professional certification
  • Career guidance and mentorship
  • Access to technical resources
  • Industry connections
  • Leadership development
  • Community engagement projects
