About IET UDSM

Who we are and why we exist

The Institution of Engineers Tanzania (IET) UDSM Student Chapter is a community dedicated to preparing engineering students for successful professional careers.

We believe that becoming an engineer is more than earning a degree. It requires practical exposure, professional ethics, leadership, networking, innovation, and continuous learning.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between university education and the engineering profession by creating opportunities that empower students long before graduation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about IET UDSM membership

Any engineering student at the University of Dar es Salaam can join IET UDSM Student Chapter. We welcome students from all engineering disciplines and year levels.

Simply create an account, fill out the application form, pay the registration fee, and wait for approval. The process typically takes 3-5 business days.

While our primary focus is on engineering students, we welcome anyone with an interest in engineering and technology to participate in our events and activities.

The annual membership fee is TZS 10,000 for students. This fee covers access to all events, workshops, and membership benefits for the entire academic year.

Members receive access to technical workshops, industrial visits, networking events, mentorship programmes, internship opportunities, and invitations to professional conferences.

Membership approval typically takes 3-5 business days after payment confirmation. You will receive an email notification once your application is approved.

As a member, you'll receive notifications about upcoming events via email and our communication channels. Simply register for events you're interested in through our platform.
